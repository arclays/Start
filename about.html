OUR COMPANY

Empowering Communities with Innovative Technology Solutions.

Arc Solutions is a technology company co-founded in 2022 by Robert Jang, a tech enthusiast from Uganda, who shared a vision of using technology to uplift Ugandan communities. The name "Arc " was inspired by the combination of "Vibe" and “Africa”, reflecting the company's mission to create positive change on the continent. At Arc solutions, we believe that technology has the power to transform lives and communities. We are committed to finding better solutions to complex problems and creating innovative technology solutions that make a positive impact on society. Our team is made up of highly skilled and experienced professionals who are passionate about creating technology solutions that make a difference.
